Professional Overview

Arunkumar Amaran is an accomplished Senior Technology Leader with over 20 years of proven expertise in architecting, developing, and delivering enterprise-scale applications across retail, healthcare, and industrial technology domains. His core focus has been on building AI-driven, cloud-native, data-centric automation frameworks that drive scalability, security, and business agility using modern architectures on GCP, AWS, and microservice ecosystems.

In his current role with Macy's Systems & Technology as a Tech Manager for Data Engineering and Architecture, he is responsible for creating and providing leadership in digital transformation initiatives such as AI-based automation of Data Engineering Pipelines, Data Insights driving customer behaviour and predictions, predictive inventory management, and AI-driven predictive observability to improve operational efficiency.

He brings prior experience from Home Depot, where he developed data-driven real-time analytics on shipments during emergencies and supply chain visibility through AI and data engineering. Across his career he has built proprietary ingestion frameworks, agentic AI systems with collaborating agents for automated code generation, and leveraged Generative AI to optimize SQL performance and significantly reduce cloud operational costs.

Beyond his technical work, Arunkumar actively contributes to the global tech community through his role as a Technical Program Committee member and reviewer for various IEEE and Springer conferences. He is passionate about the development of trustworthy, human-centric AI in line with U.S. NSTC guiding principles.

Academic & Research Interests

Generative AI for enterprise data systems, streaming and event-driven retail analytics, synthetic data generation for demand forecasting, cloud-native data pipeline automation, and AI-driven predictive observability.
